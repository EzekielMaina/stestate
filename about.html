Established in 2016, Springtide Properties Limited is accredited to the Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK), Valuation and Estate Management (VEMS) Chapter and licensed by the Estate Agents Registration Board (EARB) to offer Estate Agency Services everywhere in Kenya as per the Estate Agency Act Cap 533 of the Laws of Kenya.

In addition, our Valuations Director, Valuer Bonface Kariuki MRICS is a Chartered Valuations Surveyor with the Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors (RICS) (UK). The Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors is a global professional body promoting and enforcing the highest international standards in the valuation, management and development of land, real estate, construction and infrastructure.

About US

For more than 8 years, Springtide has undertaken numerous assignments and offered in-depth professional advice on Estate Agency, Property Valuation, Feasibility Studies, project management, Property and Facilities Management.

This sets us apart because we are able to offer the highest quality service to our clients using the globally recognized International Valuation Standards. In addition, it means that now our company can conduct valuations in all the 53 commonwealth countries and all the countries in the greater East African region.

Our staff uphold very high professional ethics and are well trained in all facets of the real estate industry ready to serve you.

We have a professional indemnity insurance cover to give our clients peace of mind.

Meet Our Team

Gloria Awinja
Managing Director.

Gloria is a real estate professional based in Kenya with over 12 years experience in the real estate business. She has a wealth of experience in property valuation, property leasing, property management and real estate consultancy.

She is the Managing Director at Springtide Properties Limited and leads a highly efficient team in carrying out property valuations, research, agency and property management and consultancy. She is responsible for overseeing the general operations at the company.

Prior to joining Springtide Properties Limited, she worked with Knight Frank Kenya (KFK) Limited, Jones Lang LaSalle (JLL) Kenya Limited, UAP Old Mutual Group, Housing Finance Company of Kenya Limited amongst other established real estate companies.

Kariuki Waweru
Valuer.

Bonface is a Chartered Valuation Surveyor (MRICS) (UK), RICS Registered Valuer, and Registered and practicing valuer in Kenya, with over 11 years of experience having worked in the public sector, private sector, and for two “Fortune 500” real estate valuation and advisory companies.

Bonface has valued some of the most prestigious properties in Kenya and 5 other African countries, including shopping malls, hotels, schools, offices, the expansive portfolio of Kenya Railways properties, industrial parks, including a fishing port on the Kenyan coast.

He is the author of 2 Kenyan Real Estate Books
“The ABC of Real Estate Investment in Kenya.”, “The Law, The Logic, The Math” 2012, revised in 2014 and “How to Invest in Real Estate in Kenya,” Everything you need to know. 2018.

Finally, he is a highly sought after expert witness in many civil cases in the Land and Environment Division of the High Court of Kenya because of his work experience and global accreditation.
