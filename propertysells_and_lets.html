Description

Springtide Properties Limited (SPL) is accredited to the Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK), Valuation and Estate Management (VEMS) Chapter and licensed by the Estate Agents Registration Board (EARB) to offer Estate Agency Services everywhere in Kenya as per the Estate Agency Act Cap 533 of the Laws of Kenya.

Incorporated in 2016, SPL is led by directors and key personnel with vast experience in property matters having being in the real estate industry for periods spanning over 15 years.
